    {% block prod_desc %}

    Kit 200 is a complete kit for facilitating the speedy process of producing popsicles.

    The kit consists of:

    1. Blender / Mixer Emulsor 15: With a capacity to beat up to 8 liters of syrup, the mixer 15 is intended for small producers who are just starting out in this business, or professionals who wish to increase their production and profitability. Its patented helix leaves the milk mixture well homogenized and with a greater incorporation of air, causing softer popsicles and more profitable. Mixer 15 incorporates 10% - 20% of air into the mixture, providing greater efficiency in production and thus increases profitability.  All these features guarantee top quality, not only in appearance, but also in texture, flavor, color, aeration, preservation, resistance to melting, and other features easily perceived by the most discerning customers. This device is easy to operate, install, and transport.

    2. Producer Popsicles Turbo 8: A semi-automatic machine that manufactures water or milk-based popsicles, working with mono-phase power - residential or commercial. The Turbo 8 helps many entrepreneurs start a promising business.  It is also designed for professionals who wish for a modular increase in production, with the ability to add more equipment as needed. This machine occupies 60% less space used by conventional equivalent equipment.  The Turbo 8 is unsurpassed when it comes to delivering successful results for novices in the ice cream industry.  It is fast, convenient, and requires no prior knowledge about making popsicles.  Its unique system allows greater production speed and is the final word in technology.  It’s easy to operate, install, and transport.

    3. Bain Marie for Chocolate: Two 20 liter tanks for dipping and coating popsicles with chocolate (skimo); can be used to extract popsicles out of the molds;  heat produced by electrical resistance; made with stainless steel AISI 304; thermostat controls water temperature of mold release.

    4. Sealing: A compact machine used in several segments.  The treadle drive leaves your hands free to work with the products.  Has the capacity seal up to a width of 20 cm. Works well with clear polypropylene plastics, laminated, bio-oriented, etc. It has several advantages, such as being extremely economical - it only requires an average of 0.30 kW / h.

    5. Moulds: Kit of molds - by Brida (www.ataforma.com.br).

     

    * Equipment with air condensation has its production reduced by 10% to 50%, from an ambient temperature above 28 ° C to 45 ° C.

     

    * *Production depends on the climatic conditions of the location where the machine is installed.
